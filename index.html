Velosity Law

AI-powered legal assistant built for small law firms handling criminal and civil cases.

What we do

Velosity simplifies and accelerates legal proceedings: early case assessments, discovery, review, and courtroom strategy. Our AI instantly surfaces relevant case law, documents, and digital evidence — saving hours of research so lawyers can spend less time searching and more time winning.

Core Offering

  • Targeted e‑Discovery: Upload case material — emails, PDFs, court records, transcripts, audio/video — and query with natural language.
  • AI‑Driven Search: Ask plain-language questions to uncover the most relevant data, people, and events in your case.
  • Smart Compilation: Automatically generate summaries, exhibits, or briefs using filters like dates, names, and incident types.
  • Minimalist Design: No bloat. Just a focused, intuitive tool for evidence analysis and legal prep.

Who it's for

  • Small Criminal & Civil Law Practices
  • Solo Attorneys and 2–10 Person Firms
  • Firms priced out of costly platforms like Relativity, Everlaw, Nuix, or Logikcull
